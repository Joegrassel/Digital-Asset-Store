## 📆 Week 1: Portfolio Power

*Goal: Showcase your best work and establish your aesthetic credentials.*

### Monday — Portfolio Spotlight
- **Template:** Instagram Post — Portfolio Showcase
- **Content:** Your most impressive recent project (interior, wedding, styling)
- **Caption:** Describe the vision, the process, and what made it special
- **Hashtags:** #[Niche]Design #Portfolio #[City]Designer
- **CTA:** "Swipe through to see every detail →"

### Wednesday — Inspiration Board
- **Platform:** Instagram Story + Pinterest pin
- **Content:** A mood board or inspiration collage
- **Goal:** Show your aesthetic point of view
- **Poll:** "Which color palette is your favorite? A) Emerald & Gold B) Blush & Ivory"

### Friday — Client Love Story
- **Template:** Instagram Story — Testimonial
- **Content:** A past client's kind words + their project photo
- **Caption:** "Why we do what we do..." + client story
- **CTA:** "Want a similar experience? Let's talk."

### Saturday — Behind the Post
- **Platform:** Instagram Reel (15 sec)
- **Content:** Time-lapse of setting up a photoshoot or project reveal
- **Goal:** Show the effort behind the beauty — authenticity wins

## 📆 Week 2: Trust & Transformation

*Goal: Build credibility through proof and social validation.*

### Monday — Transformation Tuesday
- **Template:** Instagram Post — Transformation
- **Content:** Before/after of a room makeover, event setup, or style transformation
- **Caption:** The "from blank canvas to masterpiece" story
- **Hashtags:** #TransformationTuesday #BeforeAndAfter #[Niche]Design
- **CTA:** "DM us to start your transformation"

### Wednesday — The Process
- **Template:** Instagram Story — Process / Behind the Scenes
- **Content:** Step-by-step of how you work (consultation → design → execution)
- **Goal:** Show the value of your process, not just the result
- **Poll:** "Which stage do you find most interesting?"

### Friday — Client Q&A
- **Platform:** Instagram Story + Post
- **Content:** Answer 3 common questions from clients
- **Caption:** "You ask, we answer. Here's everything you wanted to know about working with us."
- **CTA:** "Drop your questions in the comments!"

### Saturday — Vendor Tag
- **Platform:** Instagram Post
- **Content:** A shoutout to a vendor or collaborator you love
- **Caption:** "Behind every great project is an amazing team. Thank you @[Vendor]!"
- **Goal:** Build relationships + reach new audiences through tags

## 📆 Week 3: Behind the Brand

*Goal: Humanize your business and build personal connection.*

### Monday — Meet the Team
- **Template:** Instagram Post or Carousel
- **Content:** Introduce yourself or your team (photo + mini bio)
- **Caption:** "The face behind the brand 👋" — share your story, why you started, what you love
- **Hashtags:** #MeetTheTeam #SmallBusiness #BoutiqueBrand

### Wednesday — Studio/Workspace Tour
- **Platform:** Instagram Reel (20-30 sec)
- **Content:** Quick tour of your studio, office, or creative space
- **Vibe:** Warm, inviting, authentic
- **Goal:** Let clients feel like they know you
- **Music:** Trending audio — keep it light and upbeat

### Friday — Client Case Study
- **Platform:** LinkedIn Article + Instagram Carousel
- **Content:** Deep dive into one project — challenge, solution, result
- **Caption:** "The full story behind [Project Name]"
- **CTA:** "Book a discovery call to discuss your project"
- **LinkedIn focus:** Professional tone, educational value

### Saturday — Personal Post
- **Platform:** Instagram
- **Content:** Something non-work-related that shows personality (hobbies, travel, coffee, pets)
- **Goal:** Relatability — clients want to work with humans, not businesses
- **Pro tip:** This often gets the highest engagement!

## 📆 Week 4: Book Now

*Goal: Convert interest into booked projects.*

### Monday — Service Menu
- **Template:** Instagram Story — Special Offer / Service Menu
- **Content:** Your service packages with clear pricing tiers or value descriptions
- **Caption:** "How we work + what it costs 💎"
- **CTA:** "Link in bio to book your consultation"

### Wednesday — Limited Time Offer
- **Template:** Instagram Story — Special Offer
- **Content:** "Book by [Date] and receive [Bonus]"
- **Urgency:** Time-limited (seasonal, introductory, or referral)
- **CTA:** "DM 'BOOK' for details"
- **Goal:** Create a reason to act NOW

### Friday — Client Highlight Reel
- **Platform:** Instagram Carousel (5-7 slides)
- **Content:** Roundup of your best projects from the month
- **Caption:** "This month in review — thank you to our incredible clients!"
- **Hashtags:** #ClientLove #MonthInReview #[City]Designer
- **CTA:** "Ready to be next month's highlight? Let's create something beautiful."

### Saturday — "You're the Inspiration" (User-Generated Content)
- **Platform:** Instagram Story
- **Content:** Share follower's saved posts, tagged photos, or DMs
- **Repost:** A past client's post about their experience
- **Goal:** Social proof + community appreciation

## 🎯 Posting Best Practices

### For Instagram
- **Carousel posts** get 3-5x more saves than single images — use them for portfolios
- **Reels** reach non-followers — use trending audio
- **Stories** drive DMs — use polls, questions, and sliders
- **Post at peak times:** 11AM-1PM or 6-8PM weekdays

### For LinkedIn
- **Share the "why"** behind your projects, not just photos
- **Tag collaborators** (vendors, venues, photographers)
- **Use LinkedIn articles** for case studies (long-form)
- **Post 2-3x/week** — consistency matters more than frequency

### For Pinterest
- **Create 3 pins per project** (different images, same link)
- **Optimize pin titles**: "Modern [City] Wedding | [Color] Palette Inspiration"
- **Add to relevant group boards** for broader reach
- **Best schedule:** 1-2 pins/day using a scheduler
