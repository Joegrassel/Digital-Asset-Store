## 1️⃣ Profile Optimization Audit

*Your profile is your digital storefront window. First impressions happen in 3 seconds.*

### ☐ Professional Profile Photo
- [ ] **High-quality** clinic logo or professional headshot (not blurry, not a selfie)
- [ ] **Brand-consistent** colors (matches your website, signage, uniforms)
- [ ] **Cropped correctly** — 320×320px minimum, face/logo centered

### ☐ Bio That Converts
- [ ] **Clinic name** clearly stated
- [ ] **Location** (city + state, or full address if single-location)
- [ ] **Value proposition** in 1-2 lines — what makes YOU different?
- [ ] **Emojis** used strategically (not overwhelming)
- [ ] **Action-oriented CTA** — "Book your consult" / "DM for appointments"

**Good example bio:**
```
✨ Luxury aesthetics in [City]
🔬 Science-backed | Result-driven
📸 Before/afters 👇
📅 Book your consult ↓
[Link]
```

### ☐ Link in Bio
- [ ] Links to booking page (not just your website homepage)
- [ ] **OR** uses a link-in-bio tool (Linktree, Beacons, etc.) with:
  - Book Appointment
  - View Services & Pricing
  - Gallery of Results
  - Special Offers

### ☐ Story Highlights
- [ ] **Before & After** — your #1 converting content
- [ ] **Services / Treatments** — menu at a glance
- [ ] **About / The Team** — build trust and personality
- [ ] **Reviews / Testimonials** — social proof
- [ ] **Special Offers** — limited-time promos
- [ ] **FAQs** — answer common questions proactively
- [ ] Highlights have branded cover images (consistent colors/icons)

## 2️⃣ Content Mix Audit

*Variety keeps your audience engaged. Too much of one type = fatigue.*

### ☐ Educational Content (30% of posts)
- [ ] Treatment explainers ("How does [treatment] work?")
- [ ] Ingredient deep-dives ("What's in our [product]?")
- [ ] Myth-busting posts ("3 things you've been told wrong about Botox")
- [ ] "Did you know?" quick facts

### ☐ Results / Transformations (30% of posts)
- [ ] **Before/After comparisons** — your highest-converting content
- [ ] Multiple angles (front, side, close-up)
- [ ] Real results from real patients (with consent)
- [ ] Honest timelines ("Results at Week 2 vs Week 8")

### ☐ Trust & Credibility (20% of posts)
- [ ] Staff spotlights — introduce your practitioners
- [ ] Behind-the-scenes of treatments (with patient consent)
- [ ] Certifications, training, or conference attendance
- [ ] Media mentions or awards

### ☐ Promotional / Offers (20% of posts)
- [ ] Seasonal specials (holiday, summer, New Year)
- [ ] New treatment launches
- [ ] Package deals (e.g., "Buy 3, get 1 free")
- [ ] Referral incentives

**Red flags to watch for:**
- ❌ No before/afters in the last 30 days
- ❌ Only promotional content (no education)
- ❌ More than 3 posts in a row without a clear purpose

## 3️⃣ Visual Consistency Audit

*Aesthetic clinics must look premium. Your visuals = your perceived quality.*

### ☐ Color Palette
- [ ] 3-4 consistent brand colors used across all posts
- [ ] On-trend clinic colors? (blush, rose gold, sage, ivory)
- [ ] Avoid: harsh clinical whites, mismatched colors
- [ ] **Our recommendation:** Champagne Blush `#E8D5C4`, Rose Gold `#D4A574`, Sage `#B8C5B0`, Ivory `#F5F0EB`

### ☐ Typography
- [ ] 1-2 fonts used consistently
- [ ] Serif font for headings (luxury feel: Cormorant Garamond)
- [ ] Clean sans-serif for body (readability: Inter or Montserrat)
- [ ] No random font changes between posts

### ☐ Template / Layout Consistency
- [ ] Same template structure for similar post types
- [ ] Before/after layouts look professional (not cropped poorly)
- [ ] Text legible over images (use overlays or glassmorphism)
- [ ] **Our recommendation:** Dedicated templates for treatment spotlight, educational, testimonial, and offer posts

### ☐ Photography Quality
- [ ] Well-lit, professional photos
- [ ] Consistent lighting between "before" and "after" shots
- [ ] No blurry or pixelated images
- [ ] Clinic environment shots (clean, premium feel)

---

## 4️⃣ Engagement Audit

*Posting is only half the battle. You need to interact.*

### ☐ Response Time
- [ ] DMs replied to within 4 hours (Instagram marks fast-repliers)
- [ ] Comments acknowledged within 24 hours
- [ ] Auto-responder set up for FAQs

### ☐ Proactive Engagement
- [ ] Comment on 5-10 relevant accounts daily (local businesses, vendors, influencers)
- [ ] Reply to all story mentions and tags
- [ ] Engage with patient posts (likes + genuine comments)

### ☐ Community Building
- [ ] Use polls, Q&A stickers in Stories (weekly)
- [ ] Ask open-ended questions in captions
- [ ] Run "tag a friend who needs this" campaigns

---

## 5️⃣ Conversion Setup Audit

*Likes don't pay the bills. Consultations do.*

### ☐ Call-to-Action (CTA) in Every Post
- [ ] Every post has a clear next step
- [ ] Variety of CTAs: "DM to book", "Tap the link", "Call us at..."
- [ ] CTA matches the post type (e.g., "DM for pricing" on before/afters)

### ☐ Booking Funnel
- [ ] Link in bio → booking page works (click through yourself!)
- [ ] Story "Book Now" sticker or swipe-up is active
- [ ] Booking link is pinned in comments of top posts

### ☐ Lead Capture
- [ ] Offer a lead magnet? (free consultation guide, skincare quiz)
- [ ] Email signup visible (in bio, story highlights, or website)
- [ ] Retargeting pixel/audience setup for ads
